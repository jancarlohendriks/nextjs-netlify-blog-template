The table below creates an overview of my portfolio items linked to the FHICT Semester 5 competences.

<table>
  <tr>
    <th>Competence</th>
    <th>Description</th>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>1 - Knowledge and Insight</td>
    <td>
      - Knowledge and insight with the right breadth and depth of the IT life
      cycle phases: management, analysis, advice, design and realization. -
      Degree of creation of the assignment and independence and personal
      contribution that the assignment requires.
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>2a - Management</td>
    <td>
      - Working method and tooling designed to achieve professional
      implementation, suitable for assignment - Worked according to the set-up
      method and tooling
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>2b - Analysis</td>
    <td>
      - Clear definition and delineation of context, problem or challenge -
      Problem analysis with correct width and depth
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>2c - Advise</td>
    <td>
      - Correct balancing of interests, possibilities, costs and benefits - Form
      and content appropriate to the assignment and context
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>2d - Design</td>
    <td>
      - Form and content appropriate to the assignment and context - In line
      with advice and sufficiently elaborated for realisation
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>2e - Realisation</td>
    <td>
      - In line with design - In size and quality appropriate to the problem
      definition and context - Usable for client
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>3 - Judgement</td>
    <td>
      - Formulates appropriate research questions and sub-questions based on an
      unstructured practical problem, in all phases of methodical work. -
      Independently selects research methods, carries them out and justifies
      this with reference to the research strategies and can demonstrate the
      soundness of his research. - Involves social, international, scientific
      and ethical aspects in the decision-making process
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>4 - Communication</td>
    <td>
      - Accounts for and defends the implementation of a large professional
      assignment performed in a company or institution, both orally and in
      writing and adapts the writing style to the intended target group in the
      Dutch language or English language. - Collaborates effectively in the
      independent execution of a professional assignment.
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>5 - Learning ability</td>
    <td>
      - Describes his professional talents and development ambitions in relation
      to the ICT profession. - Involves self-reflection and received feedback on
      one's own functioning in the ICT profession. - Takes initiatives, acts
      independently and works independently and in a result-oriented manner on a
      professional assignment outside of school.
    </td>
  </tr>
</table>
