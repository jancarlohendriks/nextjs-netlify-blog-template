The table below creates an overview of my portfolio items linked to the FHICT Semester 5 competences.

|Competence               |Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
|-------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|1 - Knowledge and Insight|- Knowledge and insight with the right breadth and depth of the IT life cycle phases: management, analysis, advice, design and realization. - Degree of creation of the assignment and independence and personal contribution that the assignment requires.                                                                                                                                                           |
|2a - Management          |- Working method and tooling designed to achieve professional implementation, suitable for assignment - Worked according to the set-up method and tooling                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
|2b - Analysis            |- Clear definition and delineation of context, problem or challenge - Problem analysis with correct width and depth                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
|2c - Advise              |- Correct balancing of interests, possibilities, costs and benefits - Form and content appropriate to the assignment and context                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
|2d - Design              |- Form and content appropriate to the assignment and context - In line with advice and sufficiently elaborated for realisation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
|2e - Realisation         |- In line with design - In size and quality appropriate to the problem definition and context - Usable for client                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
|3 - Judgement            |- Formulates appropriate research questions and sub-questions based on an unstructured practical problem, in all phases of methodical work. - Independently selects research methods, carries them out and justifies this with reference to the research strategies and can demonstrate the soundness of his research. - Involves social, international, scientific and ethical aspects in the decision-making process|
|4 - Communication        |- Accounts for and defends the implementation of a large professional assignment performed in a company or institution, both orally and in writing and adapts the writing style to the intended target group in the Dutch language or English language. - Collaborates effectively in the independent execution of a professional assignment.                                                                         |
|5 - Learning ability     |- Describes his professional talents and development ambitions in relation to the ICT profession. - Involves self-reflection and received feedback on one's own functioning in the ICT profession. - Takes initiatives, acts independently and works independently and in a result-oriented manner on a professional assignment outside of school.                                                                    |
